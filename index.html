Welcome To The Project VλNCE Website!

Project VλNCE is a total conversion Half-Life 2 mod from the perspective of Alyx, set in an alternate universe where Gordon Freeman declined G-Man's offer at the end of Half-Life 1, and is presumed dead. Play through a unique take on the events of Half-Life 2 with new and improved environments, gameplay, combat, graphics, voice acting, and more. This is a continuation on the cancelled mod "Vance".
